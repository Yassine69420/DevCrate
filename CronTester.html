How to Use Cron Expressions

A cron expression is a string of five fields that describe individual time details. Here’s a breakdown of the structure:

Field Allowed Values Description
Minute 0-59 The minute of the hour.
Hour 0-23 The hour of the day.
Day of Month 1-31 The day of the month.
Month 1-12 The month of the year.
Day of Week 0-6 The day of the week (Sunday to Saturday).

Examples:

  • */15 * * * * - Runs every 15 minutes.
  • 0 9 * * 1-5 - Runs at 9:00 AM every weekday.
  • 30 22 1 * * - Runs at 10:30 PM on the first day of every month.
